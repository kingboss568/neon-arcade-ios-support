# NEON ARCADE Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-12

NEON ARCADE is an offline arcade game collection. The app does not require an account, does not include advertising, does not use analytics, and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Game progress, scores, settings, language preference, sound preference, and unlock state are stored locally on the user's device. The unlock-all purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. NEON ARCADE does not receive or store payment card details.

The app does not request camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, location, or tracking permission.

For support, contact Yu Shiung Jiang at jushiung@gmail.com.
